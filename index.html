FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about CloudFusion Pro.

Is my data really secure?
Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption, the same standard adopted by the U.S. government. Your files are encrypted both in transit and at rest on our servers. We also offer two-factor authentication for added security.
How does the unlimited storage work?
Our Enterprise plan offers truly unlimited storage with no hidden caps or throttling. You can store as much as you need without worrying about running out of space. We monitor for abuse to ensure fair usage for all customers.
Can I access my files offline?
Yes! Our desktop and mobile apps allow you to mark files for offline access. Any changes you make will automatically sync when you reconnect to the internet.
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You'll continue to have access to your files for 30 days after cancellation. After that period, your account will be downgraded to a free plan with limited storage (5GB), and you'll need to remove files exceeding that limit.
Do you offer team plans?
Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes team management features. You can add team members, control permissions, and monitor usage all from a central dashboard.
How fast are your upload/download speeds?
We optimize speeds based on your location and internet connection. Our global network of servers ensures fast performance worldwide. Most users experience maximum speeds limited only by their internet connection.
